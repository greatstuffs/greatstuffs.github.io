We specialize on selling sea shells and other sea shore treasures. We sell shark jaws as well but all of these items that are posted here are all by-products from human consumption and to save the earth we create, reuse and recycle. And by doing that we have expenses to pay therefore we sell it at a very low and very affordable prices which means from us direct to you. Most of our products are from the pearl of the orient country in the Philippines.
We can assure you that nothing is endanger species because before we export these products from the Phili we acquired Export Commodity Clearance and taxes were paid off.
We aim to share and be proud off to show to you most of the items that reflects the beauty of a certain country where our products came from. We also sell antiquity items especially from the west country in the United Kingdom.
